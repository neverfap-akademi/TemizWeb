TemizWeb social UI v7.4

YouTube
- Homepage recommendation feed remains removed.
- Shorts navigation entry remains removed.
- Homepage Shorts shelves/cards are removed only on the root homepage.
- Search Shorts, channel Shorts tabs/cards, and direct Shorts playback are allowed.
- Subscriptions navigation and the followed-channels feed are restored.

Social account filtering
- Ordinary post-level rules hide only the risky tweet/post/video card.
- Account-result cards use a separate identity threshold.
- Strong explicit identities such as "seksi kızlar", "nude women",
  leak-person names, and NSFW-media identities may be hidden.
- Shared recovery/legal/education exceptions still apply.
- Composer, upload, Studio and publishing surfaces are not targeted.
