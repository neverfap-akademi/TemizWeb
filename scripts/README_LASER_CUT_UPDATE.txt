TemizWeb v7 — laser-cut social account policy

Post-level PMO rules target only the smallest tweet, video, post or reel card.
They no longer reuse account-result containers.

Account-result cards are handled separately and require a much higher threshold:
- a direct explicit identity, or
- leak + person + media, or
- nudity + person + media, or
- sexualized + person + media.

Therefore a normal account with one risky post remains visible; only that post is hidden.
Recovery, legal/help, educational, upload, composer and publishing guards remain intact.
